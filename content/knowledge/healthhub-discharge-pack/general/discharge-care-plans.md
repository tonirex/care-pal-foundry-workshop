# Support & Care Programmes — Discharge Care Plans

A discharge care plan helps you continue recovery safely at home. It lists your medicines, follow-up
appointments, warning signs, and who to call for help.

## Your plan usually covers
- Medicines and when to take them; follow-up clinic dates.
- Daily self-monitoring (e.g. weight, blood pressure, symptoms).
- Red-flag symptoms and the right action — call your care team, or 995 for emergencies.
- Community supports: home care, rehab, and caregiver help.

## Make it work
- Keep the plan where you can see it; bring it to appointments.
- Ask a caregiver to help with reminders and check-ins.

## When to seek help
Use your plan's contacts for non-urgent concerns. For chest pain, severe breathlessness, fainting or
stroke signs, call 995 or go to A&E.
