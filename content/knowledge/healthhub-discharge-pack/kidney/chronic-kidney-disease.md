# Chronic Kidney Disease

Chronic kidney disease (CKD) means the kidneys filter waste less well over time. Good daily habits
slow it down and protect your heart.

## Daily care
- Take blood-pressure and kidney medicines as prescribed; keep your follow-up appointments.
- Eat less salt; your team may advise limits on potassium, phosphate or fluids.
- Stay active, keep a healthy weight, and do not smoke.

## Watch for
- Swelling, very tired feeling, poor appetite, or much less urine.
- Avoid painkillers like NSAIDs and unverified herbal supplements — they can harm kidneys.

## When to seek help
Contact your care team if swelling or tiredness worsens. For severe breathlessness, confusion, or
chest pain, call 995 or go to A&E.
