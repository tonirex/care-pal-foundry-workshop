# What is Peritoneal Dialysis

> Synthetic workshop sample, mirroring the customer's cited HealthHub article. Replace with the
> verified export before go-live. Not medical advice.

Peritoneal dialysis (PD) cleans the blood using the lining of your abdomen. A cleansing fluid flows
in through a soft tube, absorbs waste, then drains out. Many people do PD at home.

## Daily care
- Wash hands and use clean technique for every exchange to prevent infection.
- Keep the catheter exit site clean and dry; check for redness, pain or discharge.
- Track weight and blood pressure; follow fluid and diet advice.

## Watch for
- Cloudy drain fluid, tummy pain, or fever may mean infection (peritonitis) — contact your team early.

## When to seek help
Call your care team for infection signs. For severe breathlessness or chest pain, call 995 or A&E.
