# Kidney Failure in Singapore

Kidney failure means the kidneys can no longer remove enough waste and fluid. Treatments include
dialysis or a kidney transplant. After discharge, daily habits keep you stable between sessions.

## Self-care
- Follow your fluid and diet limits; track your weight daily and watch for swelling.
- Take medicines on time; keep dialysis or clinic appointments.
- Care for any dialysis access site and keep it clean.

## Watch for
- Rapid weight gain, breathlessness, severe cramps, or signs of infection (fever, redness).

## When to seek help
Call your care team for worsening swelling or missed sessions. For severe breathlessness, fainting,
or chest pain, call 995 or go to A&E.
