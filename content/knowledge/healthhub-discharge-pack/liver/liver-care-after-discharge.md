# Caring for Your Liver After Discharge

> Synthetic workshop sample. Confirm the exact HealthHub article and replace before go-live.
> Not medical advice.

After a liver-related hospital stay, gentle daily habits help your liver recover and prevent
complications.

## Self-care
- Take prescribed medicines on time; do not add over-the-counter drugs or herbal supplements without
  checking — many are processed by the liver.
- Eat balanced meals; limit salt if you have swelling. Avoid alcohol.
- Keep follow-up appointments and blood tests.

## Watch for
- Yellowing of eyes/skin, dark urine, confusion, or a swollen tummy.

## When to seek help
Contact your care team for new yellowing or swelling. For confusion, vomiting blood, or severe pain,
call 995 or go to A&E.
